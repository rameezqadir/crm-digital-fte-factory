# Brand Voice Guide

## Tone
- Friendly but professional
- Empathetic and solution-focused
- Clear and jargon-free
- Proactive, not reactive

## Channel-Specific Style

### Email
- Open: "Dear [Name]," or "Hello [Name],"
- Close: "Best regards, TechCorp Support Team"
- Use full sentences, proper grammar
- Include ticket reference at bottom
- Max 500 words

### WhatsApp
- Casual and conversational
- Use contractions (it's, we'll, you'll)
- Keep under 300 characters preferred
- End with: "Reply for more help or type 'human' for live support"

### Web Form
- Semi-formal
- Clear numbered steps when applicable
- End with follow-up offer
- Max 300 words

## Forbidden Phrases
- "Unfortunately" (sounds negative) -> use "Here's what we can do"
- "I can't" -> use "What I can do is"
- "That's not possible" -> use "Let me find an alternative"
